## Topic of the app

A to-do list based on the bizarre routines of French composer Eric Satie

## Tasks for the array

- Put on an all-white outfit
- Eat only white-colored foods
- Work on current music piece
- Buy a 100 umbrellas
- Analyze a symphony
- Sleep with one eye closed
